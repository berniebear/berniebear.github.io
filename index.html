School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University. poyaoh@cs.cmu.edu

Greetings! I am Po-Yao (Bernie) Huang, a CS PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University. I am advised by Prof. Alexander G. Hauptmann. My research interests is in multimodal machine learning. I am particularly interested in bridging computer vision and natural language processing for the tasks of multimodal machine translation, cross-modal search and retrieval, and large-scale multimodal data mining and analysis.
